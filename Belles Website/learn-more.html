Practice

Our main season is in the spring but we practice year around!
Our typical practice schedule for each season is as follows:
Fall: 3x/week for 2 hours on the IM fields
Winter: 3x/week for 2 hours in the CSU indoor practice facility or Glenn Morris field house and/or gym
Spring: 3x/week for 2 hours plus an additional pod workout (~1 hour) with a small group of your teammates.

Tournaments

We typically travel to one tournament in the fall and two to three in the spring. The tournaments we've attended in the past are:
Missouri Loves Company: Columbia, Missouri - September
President's Day Invite: San Diego, California - February
Centex: Austin, Texas - March
Sectionals: Fort Collins, Colorado - April
Regionals: must qualify, (typically Colorado, Texas, Oklahoma) - April
Nationals: must qualify, (typically Ohio, Wisconsin, California, Colorado) - May

Contact Us

If you want to get in touch with a captain or someone on leadership, please email csuhellsbelles@gmail.com or DM us on Instagram @hellsbellesulti. We are very active on the email and will get back to you within 5 business days! If for some reason we don't, things get lost sometimes, please follow up again, we'd love to talk to you!

Donate!

We are fortunate enough to travel for our tournaments and participate in multiple tournaments a year; however, this can be costly to college students. In order to be successful, we must do a considerable amount of outside fundraising to support our team.
Please consider donating to help support our team. Your contribution will help pay for tournament fees, transportation, and lodging to these tournaments making it possible for our players to compete.
