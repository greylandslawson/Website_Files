Hell Yeah,
Hell's Belles!

We are CSU's women and non-binary ultiamte frisbee team. We currently have 21 members that play on the team and compete throughout the school year against other college club ultimate teams. We operate under the governing body USA Ultimate and travel to tournaments across the country like Wisconsin, Texas, California, Missouri, Oklahoma, and Ohio.

History

Our team was founded in 2002 and although our team is relatively small compared to other top level teams in the US, our success is growing every year. In the 2016-17 season, we were ranked #17 breaking into the top 20 in the nation for the second time in program history. In 2022, our team beat Texas Melee in the game to go at Regionals in Colorado making it the first time Belles have made it to Nationals in program history. The following season, Belles earned a bid during the regular season and qualified for Nationals for the second time in 2023. At this tournament, Belles placed 9th which is the best standing we've ever earned. Most recently in 2024, Belles earned a bid and qualified for Nationals and hope to continue to succeed and improve in Madison May 24-27.

Who Are We?

Beyond ultimate frisbee, this program provides student with an excellent opportunity for leadership, responsibility, community, and quality athletics that will benefit them long after they graduate. One of our club goals is to cultivate a welcoming, supportive, and positive community for students to be a part of.
